#### Anchors
* `^ Start of line`
* `$ End of line`
* `\b` Start or end of word

#### Escape Character
* `\ is the escape character`

#### Characters
* `\d Digit`
* `\D Non-Digit`
* `\w Word Character`
* `\W Non-Word Character`
* `\s Whitespace Character`
* `\S Non-Whitespace Character`

#### Character Classes
* `. matches anything except line breaking`
* `[a-e]` matches a through e
* `^[4-6]` doesn't matc
